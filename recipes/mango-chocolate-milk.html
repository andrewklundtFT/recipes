Mango Chocolate Milk Recipe

Description

A delicious recipe for mango chocolate milk

Ingredients

  • 1/2 mango
  • a glass of milk
  • 1 spoonful of chocolate
  • 2 spoonsful of sugar

Steps

  1. Combine all of the ingredients but milk in a small blender
  2. Fill the remaining space with milk
  3. Blend it up
  4. Serve!
